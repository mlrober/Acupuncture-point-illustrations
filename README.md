## Yuan Source Points
Nature
- Root / constitutional points
- Closest access to original Qi

Function
- Stores Energy and Strengthen the organ at its root
- Anchors QI and Prevent Leakage
- Builds the Energy

Important Note:
- Yuan Source Points of Yang organs serves as yin
- Source Points of Yang are opposite effect as they
	- move QI out 
	- release stagnation
	- Except Tw4 which tonifies Original QI
